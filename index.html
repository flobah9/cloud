Answers

  1. What the image does?

    We choose Ubuntu image. Ubuntu provides a stable and secure foundation for running a variety of applications on your Droplet. Ubuntu comes in different editions, each tailored for specific purposes. On DigitalOcean, you'll likely encounter the Ubuntu Server edition, optimized for running server-side applications. Being open-source, Ubuntu offers a high level of customization. You have full control over the software packages you install and how you configure the system.

  2. Why we chose it?

    Ubuntu is completely free to use and distribute. This makes it a cost-effective option for personal projects or small businesses. The open-source nature also allows for a high degree of customization and access to the underlying code. While Ubuntu caters to power users, it has become increasingly user-friendly over the years. The installation process is relatively straightforward, and the desktop environment offers a familiar and intuitive experience for those accustomed to modern operating systems.
